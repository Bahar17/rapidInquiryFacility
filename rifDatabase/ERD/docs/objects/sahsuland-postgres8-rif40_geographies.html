Table: rif40.rif40_geographies
Pre-defined hierarchial geographies. Usually based on Census geography. Beware of the foreign key from RIF40_GEOGRAPHIES.DEFAULTCOMPAREA to RIF40_GEOLEVELS. When inserting a new geography do NOT set DEFAULTCOMPAREA, update it after adding geolevels to T_RIF40_GEOLEVELS
Size: medium, Select frequency: medium, Update frequency: medium
